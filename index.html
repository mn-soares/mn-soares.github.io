Matheus N. Soares

I am currently a post-doc in Universidade Federal de Pernambuco (UFPE) under supervision of professor Fábio Reis dos Santos. My research area is differential geometry with particular interest in minimal immersions and eigenvalue estimates of differential operator in Riemannian manifolds.
